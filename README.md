 Supply Chain & Supplier Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

Overview

This project presents a Supply Chain dashboard built in Power BI to analyze procurement performance, supplier efficiency, and risk.

The dashboard focuses on supplier evaluation and procurement optimization, inspired by SAP S/4HANA Material Management (MM) processes.

---

 Objectives

- Analyze supplier performance using multiple KPIs
- Evaluate procurement cost and efficiency
- Monitor delivery performance and delays
- Identify supplier risks and optimization opportunities
- Support strategic sourcing decisions

---

 Dashboard Features

 Procurement Analysis
- Total Procurement Cost
- Average Unit Price
- Total Quantity Ordered

 Supplier Performance
- On-Time Delivery %
- Average Delivery Delay
- Supplier Score (Performance Evaluation)

 Risk Analysis
- Supplier Risk Score
- Cost vs Risk comparison

---

 Visualizations

- Procurement Cost by Supplier
- Supplier Ranking by Performance Score
- Delivery Delay by Supplier
- Cost vs Risk (Scatter Analysis)

---

 Interactivity

- Filters (Slicers) for:
  - Supplier
  - Country
  - Material
  - Date

---

 Key Insights

- Suppliers with lower delivery delays perform better overall
- Some suppliers have higher costs but lower risk
- Procurement costs vary significantly across suppliers
- Supplier evaluation helps balance cost, quality, and reliability
